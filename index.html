1. 概述

MedTime 药准时（以下简称"本应用"）尊重并保护您的隐私。本政策说明我们如何处理您在使用本应用过程中产生的信息。

本应用不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Overview

MedTime ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information generated while you use the App.

Core principle: The App does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal data. All data is stored locally on your device only.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information. All content you enter (medication information, medication logs, hydration and urination records, etc.) is stored solely in the local database on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device.
  • We do not operate any servers; data is never transmitted over the network.
  • Data security is provided by your device's operating system (iOS encryption).
  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all data.

4. Permissions

  • Notifications: Used solely to send medication reminders.
  • Local Network: Not used.
  • Location: Not used.
  • Camera / Photo Library: Not used.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will notify you within the App and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
